CONVOCATION
6TH Annual Convocation at EASTC

The 6th Convocation of EASTC of the year 2023 continues the tradition within academic institutions of gathering together academicians, Students, Alumni, Benefactors and industry professionals as stake- holders of the university community. It is the time to exchange views on topical issues that touch teaching, Research and professional development
This yeaar's convocation has selected the topic "The role of official statistics and big data in the economic transformation and sustainable development of Tanzania" as the standing point to reflect on the theme of this year's 18 of November African Statistics Day
